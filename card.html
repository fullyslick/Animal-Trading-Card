Toucan

Toucans have larger bills, in comparison to the size of their body, than any other bird in the world.
  • Scientific Name:Toucan
  • Kingdom:Animalia
  • Family:Ramphastidae
  • Habitat:Central America, South America, and the Caribbean.

Toucans are well known for their big colorful bills. There are over forty species of toucans found in the tropical rainforest of South America. Their bright colorful markings provide them with excellent camouflage in the rainforest. Toucans live in flocks made up of approximately five to six birds.
